I offer a detailed pricing menu for both packages and standalone services — ask me to send it during our discovery call and I'll walk you through what makes sense for your situation.

Most local businesses I work with have a monthly ad budget of $1,000–$5,000, which is a healthy range to work with.

Agencies charge $2,000–$5,000+/month in management fees, often assign your account to junior staff after the pitch, and spread attention across dozens of clients. You're paying for overhead, office space, and account management layers — not expertise.

With me, your monthly fee goes directly toward the work being done on your account by someone with 8+ years of experience managing $25M+ in ad spend. You get more senior attention at a fraction of the cost.
